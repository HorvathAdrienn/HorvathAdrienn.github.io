About the author

This site's author is Horváth Adrienn Mária, a webdeveloper and UX designer who lives at Hungary. Being a webdeveloper and designer means so much to her, that she has always thought it is very creative. You have to be really flexible to solve problems through coding.

Adrienn was 13 years old when started her first website. The idea was a game where you could raise horses. It has been solved by random pictures beautifully aligned, with needs like food or water. It used eoldal.hu as hosting page. Later took a 180 degrees turn and made another website with gportal.hu. The author realized that writing is a beautiful art and wanted to create a place to share her ideas. CSS designs have been used, and by seeing how the website changes, Adrienn knew what path to take.

Attended to BKSZC Pogány Frigyes Szakgimnázium in 2013, finished it as computer system administrator. Then took another two years learning as a software-developer. Softwares did not entertain her, but still has seen something in webdevelopment. So mainly self-taught the author has begun her road, created multiplie projects and is dedicated to learn something new everyday.
